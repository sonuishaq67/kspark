5 minute readiness brief

Prepare your story before answering.

This is intentionally not a script. It helps the candidate organize their own experience.

  • 1
    Prepare one backend project story.

    Be ready to explain what you built, what you owned, and what changed because of your work.

  • 2
    Review database fundamentals.

    Expect follow-ups around schema, indexes, caching, and scale trade-offs.

  • 3
    Use STAR for behavioral answers.

    Situation, task, action, result. Keep the focus on your specific contribution.

  • 4
    Avoid memorized answers.

    RoleReady will coach structure, but will not ghostwrite polished scripts.
