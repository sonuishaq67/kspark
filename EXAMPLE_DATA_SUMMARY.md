## How to Use

1. Navigate to `/practice/setup`
2. Scroll to "Context (Optional)" section
3. Click **📝 Load Example** to auto-fill:
   - Resume (3-year SDE profile)
   - Job Description (Google Senior SDE role)
   - Company: "Google"
   - Role: "Senior SDE"
   - Focus Area: "distributed systems and scalability"
4. Click **🗑 Clear All** to reset all fields
